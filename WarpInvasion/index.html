WARP INVASION
FOR WINDOWS, ANDROID

About

WP is a 2D side-view arena platformer built for Windows PC.
It features a companion app available for all Android devices.

Challenge

The project was developed at IADE, Lisbon during my Erasmus semester abroad. My team, consisting of myself and 2 classmates, took on the challenge and worked four months constantly to create an awesome product.

Overview

2D side-view arena platformer where you survive in waves and build the ultimate weapons as you go. The companion app features daily and weekly challenges that once completed will unlock special skins and game modes.

Story and Artwork

The protagonist, Sgt. Froggie struggles to survive as long as possible in a universe while fighting hordes of mutant reddishes. The Artwork was procured on the Unity asset store.

My Role

As the team leader I worked on both the app and the game, but also on documentation and planning. I contributed the most in the app as I had prior experience, but after finishing it in record time I also took on tasks in the game like the shooting aspect, enemy AI and the UI/menus.

Technologies

The game itself was developed in Unity, while the companion app was built natively in Kotlin. The server connecting the 2 parts was written in NodeJs and features and SQL Database.

Planning & Documentation

The project was vigorously planned before development and broken into backlogs and 13 sprints, that we kept track of using ClickUp and creating weekly reports and plans.

Challenging

Enemies spawn randomly on the map in waves and you have to defeat before advancing to the next level.
The smart AI will make the enemies follow and attack you without a second thought.

Upgrade Your Gun

Defeating enemies gives you modifiers that, based on your "luck factor" can upgrade your weapon's stats, either in a positive or negative way.
